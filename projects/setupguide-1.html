Context

Today, 500k businesses use WhatsApp on partner platforms like AiSensy, Twilio to manage their customers with advanced tools. However, after signup, 60% of businesses fail to complete WhatsApp setup on partner platforms, leaving messaging unused and creating a $XM revenue gap.

Discover Problems

Integration with partner platforms requires multiple setup steps. Most users rely on step-by-step guidance to complete the setup. However, research shows that 90% of WhatsApp partners lack this support, creating integration challenges.

*9 out of top 10 partners lack linear setup guidance

Clarify Ambiguity

To build the right solution, we needed to answer two questions:

  • What does the setup path look like? Steps, sequence, and required tools.
  • Who should build the guidance? Meta or partner platforms?

Understanding Gap for Setup Path

WhatsApp integration involves setup steps owned by 5+ product teams and spread across multiple Meta and partner platforms. As a result, no one had a clear view of the end-to-end setup journey.



To drive clarity on setup steps, I led cross-functional efforts with internal and partner teams to map the end-to-end setup journey.



Through cross-disciplinary research, we identified insights that informed key decisions:

  • Sequence: Account → tool testing (two core tools) → profile setup
  • Minimum path: 6 tasks required to complete account setup and testing
  • User preference: Less experienced users prefer a linear guide over multiple options
