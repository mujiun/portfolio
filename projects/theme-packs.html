Texture of Leaf

Green color is the color of the year in 2017, "Texture of Leaf" launched with the ZenFone Deluxe which possesses the newest display techniques (AMOLED) and could represent the details and high contrast colors of the images, users could find the clear texture of leaf and grab the fresh plants in their hands.

The green color tone was modified for numerous times, aiming to find the best result that could show on AMOLED screen.
