Discover Problems

What we found

Data Insights

After signing up, 60% of businesses never send a single WhatsApp message through partner tools

Data Insights

No message sent = onboarding incomplete = not yet a user

Think first Uber ride or first TikTok publish — the moment a new signup becomes a real user

Pain Points

Identified 7 onboarding frictions through user research — prioritized 3 to solve first

Informed decision with XFN and x-team — considered impact, eng cost, and dependency

  1. Budget: Pricing concerns or internal approval needed
  2. Tools: Features don't meet business needs
  3. Guidance: No onboarding guide or partner support
  4. Awareness: Unclear on WhatsApp use cases
  5. Account: Blocked accounts or failed verification
  6. Integration: Third-party app setup issues
  7. Sign up: Permission or phone registration errors

Product Gap

Users struggle with non-linear onboarding resources, which most WhatsApp partners offer

9 out of 10 partners offer onboarding resources this way

Product Gap

Users want linear guidance showing sequential steps to guide their account and tool setup

Example of TikTok Shop onboarding guide on Shopify

Monetization Gap

Onboarded businesses mostly use free messaging not paid products, leading to a monetization gap
