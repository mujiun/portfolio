WhatsApp Setup Guide for Businesses

0-1 Opportunity/Product, Standardized Setup Path

Problems

After signup, 60% of businesses fail to complete WhatsApp setup on partner platforms.

Solutions

Developed a linear step-by-step guidance that drive integration succeed and product adoption.

My Role

  • Secured funding from leadership
  • Led strategy/designs in full product cycle
  • Drove alignment across 5 teams

Impact

  • +9% integration completion rate
  • +19.7% revenue growth, $43M-$107M annual impact est.

Context

Over 500k businesses use WhatsApp on partner platforms like AiSensy, Twilio to manage their customers with advanced tools.

User Problems

Integration with partner platforms requires multiple setup steps. Most users rely on step-by-step guidance to complete the setup. However, research shows that 90% of WhatsApp partners lack linear guidance, creating integration challenges.

*9 out of top 10 partners lack linear setup guidance

Insights

After signup, 60% of businesses fail to complete WhatsApp setup on partner platforms.

Gaps for Monetization

Integration challenges delay or block WhatsApp adoption, there's also a gap to adopt paid message and ad products.

Clarify Ambiguity

WhatsApp integration involves setup steps owned by 5+ product teams and spread across multiple Meta and partner platforms. As a result, no one had a clear view of the end-to-end setup journey.

To drive clarity on setup steps, I led cross-functional efforts with internal and partner teams to map the end-to-end setup journey.

Key Insights

Through cross-disciplinary research, we identified insights that informed key decisions:

  • Sequence: Account → tool testing (two core tools) → profile setup
  • Minimum path: 6 tasks required to complete account setup and testing
  • User preference: Less experienced users prefer a linear guide over multiple options
