Today's Problem

Businesses have no way to message existing customers at scale through Meta Business Inbox. They can only reply one by one — so most leads never get a follow-up, and businesses turn to third-party tools instead.

63%

conversations with no business follow-up within 24 hours

2.4×

higher conversion when businesses follow up same day

41%

of advertisers use third-party tools for re-engagement

Proposal

Build a bulk messaging tool within Meta Business Inbox that enables businesses to easily re-engage existing customers through targeted follow-ups — driving conversations to conversion without leaving the platform.
